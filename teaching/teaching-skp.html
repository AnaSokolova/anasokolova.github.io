Ana Sokolova: Teaching at II, PMF, Skopje


From 1995 till 2001, I was developing and giving instructions in the following courses at the Institute of Informatics , Faculty of Natural Sciences and Mathematics in Skopje:
  • Introduction to Computer Science for students of the departments of Biology, Chemistry and Geography
  • Introduction to Computer Science (Pascal programming) for first year Computer Science students
  • Databases for 6th semester Computer Science students
  • Sets and logic for 1st semester Computer Science students
  • Algebra 1 for 4th semester Mathematics students
  • Algebra 2 for third year Mathematics students
  • Mathematical Logic and Algebra for second year Computer Science students
  • Algebraic discrete structures for third year Computer Science students
  • Algorithms and automata for 8th semester Computer Science students
